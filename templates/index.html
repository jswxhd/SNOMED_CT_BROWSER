SNOMED CT -- Systematized Nomenclature of Medicine-Clinical Terms, is a clinical terminology that is widely used internationally. This set of terminology provides a comprehensive and unified medical terminology system. This website provides accurate search in Snomed CT terminology database. Searching results are optimized by the algorithm. You can search and learn medical terms in the website. Enjoy your search!
